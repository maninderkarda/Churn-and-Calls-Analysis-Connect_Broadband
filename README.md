# Connect Broadband Customer Churn & Call Center Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI project was developed for Connect Broadband, an internet service provider, to analyze customer churn behavior and customer support performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights into customer retention, revenue loss, service quality, and call center operations, helping stakeholders improve customer experience and business performance.

The solution combines Customer Churn Analytics and Call Center Analytics into a single reporting platform, enabling business users to identify churn drivers, evaluate support efficiency, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and business growth for telecom and broadband companies. Additionally, customer support teams need visibility into service performance and issue resolution effectiveness.

The organization required a centralized dashboard to:

* Understand why customers are leaving.
* Identify customer segments with high churn risk.
* Quantify revenue loss due to churn.
* Monitor customer support performance.
* Evaluate agent productivity and effectiveness.
* Improve customer satisfaction and retention.

---

## Solution

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard consisting of two analytical pages:

### Churn Analysis Dashboard

Provides insights into:

* Customer Churn Trends
* Churn Percentage Analysis
* Revenue Loss Analysis
* Customer Demographics
* Payment Method Analysis
* Tenure-Based Churn Analysis
* Internet Service Performance

### Call Analysis Dashboard

Provides visibility into:

* Call Center Performance
* Resolution Rates
* Agent Productivity
* Customer Satisfaction
* Call Topic Analysis
* Response Time Monitoring
* Support Efficiency

## Key Business Questions Answered

### Customer Churn Analysis

* What percentage of customers are churning?
* Which customer segments have the highest churn rate?
* Does customer tenure influence churn behavior?
* Which payment methods are associated with higher churn?
* Which internet services experience the highest churn?
* How much monthly revenue is lost due to customer churn?

### Call Center Analytics

* What percentage of customer issues are resolved?
* Which call topics generate the highest call volumes?
* What is the average speed of answer?
* Which agents achieve the highest resolution rates?
* How satisfied are customers with support services?
* What factors contribute to unresolved cases?
